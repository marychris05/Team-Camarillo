Discover recommended beaches and destinations

From crystal-clear waters to powdery white sands — explore some of the most breathtaking beaches across the Philippines.

🏖️ Maribago Public Beach

A serene getaway in Cebu where you can swim for free and enjoy the soft sand under the tropical sun.

🌅 Nacpan Beach

Golden sands and calm blue waters make this El Nido gem perfect for swimming and sunset views.

🌊 Puka Shell Beach

Located in Boracay’s northern tip, this tranquil spot is known for its natural seashells and quiet charm.
